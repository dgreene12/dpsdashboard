This project is rooted in the Bass Connections team from Duke University and North Carolina Central University. The group is committed to creating more responsible and imaginative relationships between the universities and Durham Public Schools.


This website aims to provide a centralized tool for those entering Durham Public Schools. In addition, it hopes to inform university students of outreach opportunities and support educators in picturing their community.


Duke and NCCU both aim to foster partnerships with Durham Public Schools. Previous team research found that volunteers lacking knowledge of city and community relations is a major block to positive relations between universities and public schools. Additionally, they found a lack of student volunteer training specific to Durham's history. A better understanding of Durham's people and power dynamics in schools is necessary for those entering its system.


Motivated by these findings, our team continues to explore ways to visualize schools as community centers to bring together development and engagement, health and social services, and schools in Durham.
